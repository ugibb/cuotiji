标准库（生产）PBL_STD 预处理库（版权隔离）PBL_PREP 项目制学习 + 错题执行闭环

0. 域前缀命名规范

前缀域说明代表表
usr_ 身份账号 家长与学生档案 usr_users, usr_students
knl_ 知识与题库 知识体系主干 + 题库（静态内容） knl_modules, knl_topics, knl_skills, knl_questions, knl_chapters
onb_ 评测 + 能力画像 诊断测评、能力报告、技能掌握度 onb_ability_assessments, onb_question_pools, onb_skill_mastery
stu_ 学生执行 每日作业、错题、对话、打卡 stu_assignments, stu_problems, stu_ai_dialogues
pla_ 项目与计划 项目实例、冲刺计划、里程碑 pla_learning_projects, pla_sprint_plans, pla_milestones
pre_ 预处理库（隔离） 版权原材料解析、人工审核、同步闸门 pre_source_lessons, pre_questions, pre_lecture_kp

1. 用例口径确认

项确认结果本 ER 口径
知识点粒度 7大板块 + 章节 + 知识点 knl_modules(7板块) → knl_topics(章节) → knl_skills(知识点)
解题方法 一题多解 knl_question_solutions，method_seq 区分方法1..n
AI 对话 统一可行，其他数据关联对话ID 统一表 stu_ai_dialogues，context_type 区分场景
评测题库 规则筛选 + 显式题池并存 onb_question_pools + onb_pool_question_map
能力画像 归入评测域管理 onb_skill_mastery, onb_topic_scores
双库隔离 标准库 + 预处理库分开部署 PBL_STD / PBL_PREP，小程序只访问标准库

2. ER 关系总览（文本拓扑）

── 标准库 (PBL_STD) ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────

[usr_] 身份
  usr_users 1 ── N usr_students

[knl_] 知识与题库
  knl_systems 1 ── N knl_modules 1 ── N knl_topics 1 ── N knl_skills
  knl_topics  1 ── N knl_chapters          (兼容层)
  knl_questions N ── N knl_skills          (via knl_question_skill_map)
  knl_questions 1 ── N knl_question_solutions

[onb_] 评测 + 能力画像
  onb_question_pools N ── N knl_questions  (via onb_pool_question_map)
  pla_learning_projects 1 ── 1 onb_ability_assessments
  onb_ability_assessments 1 ── N onb_assessment_sessions
  onb_assessment_sessions 1 ── N onb_assessment_results
  onb_assessment_results N ── 1 knl_questions
  onb_assessment_results N ── 1 knl_skills
  usr_students N ── N knl_skills           (via onb_skill_mastery)
  usr_students N ── N knl_topics           (via onb_topic_scores)

[pla_] 项目与计划
  usr_students  1 ── N pla_learning_projects
  pla_types     1 ── N pla_learning_projects
  pla_learning_projects 1 ── 1 pla_sprint_plans
  pla_sprint_plans 1 ── N pla_milestones
  pla_milestones 1 ── 1 pla_milestone_retros
  pla_learning_projects 1 ── 1 pla_project_retros

[stu_] 学生执行
  pla_sprint_plans 1 ── N stu_training_plans
  stu_training_plans 1 ── N stu_training_plan_items
  stu_training_plan_items N ── 1 knl_skills
  stu_training_plan_items N ── 0..1 knl_questions
  usr_students 1 ── N stu_assignments
  stu_assignments 1 ── N stu_problems
  stu_problems N ── N knl_skills          (via stu_wrong_answer_skill_map)
  stu_assignments 1 ── 1 stu_review_sessions
  usr_students 1 ── N stu_ai_dialogues    (context_type + context_id)
  usr_students 1 ── N stu_daily_check_ins

── 预处理库 (PBL_PREP) ───────────────────────────────────────────────────

  pre_source_lessons 1 ── N pre_lecture_methods
  pre_source_lessons 1 ── N pre_lecture_kp
  pre_source_lessons 1 ── N pre_questions
  pre_material_topics 1 ── N pre_questions
  pre_questions N ── N pre_lecture_kp     (via pre_question_kp_map)
  pre_questions N ── 1 knl_questions      (sync_to_std after review_status=approved)

3. 库边界与同步闸门

库用途代表表对小程序可见
预处理库 PBL_PREP 教材解析、题目提取、人工审核 pre_source_lessons, pre_questions, pre_lecture_kp 不可见
标准库 PBL_STD 小程序在线业务与题库服务 knl_questions, stu_assignments, pla_milestones, onb_skill_mastery 可见（小程序只连标准库）

同步条件：pre_questions.review_status = 'approved' 且 stem_latex IS NOT NULL 才可同步到 knl_questions。

4. 分域关系速查

域核心表关键关系
usr_ 身份账号 usr_users, usr_students usr_users(1) → usr_students(N)
knl_ 知识与题库 knl_modules, knl_topics, knl_skills, knl_chapters, knl_questions, knl_question_skill_map, knl_question_solutions modules→topics→skills；questions(N)↔skills(N)
onb_ 评测 + 画像 onb_ability_assessments, onb_assessment_sessions, onb_assessment_results, onb_question_pools, onb_pool_question_map, onb_skill_mastery, onb_topic_scores project(1)→assessment(1)→sessions(N)→results(N)；students(N)↔skills(N)掌握度
pla_ 项目与计划 pla_types, pla_learning_projects, pla_sprint_plans, pla_milestones, pla_milestone_retros, pla_project_retros student(1)→projects(N)→sprint_plan(1)→milestones(N)
stu_ 学生执行 stu_training_plans, stu_training_plan_items, stu_assignments, stu_problems, stu_dialogues, stu_ai_dialogues, stu_review_sessions, stu_wrong_answer_skill_map, stu_daily_check_ins sprint_plan→training_plans→items；assignments→problems→wrong_answer_skill_map
pre_ 预处理库 pre_source_lessons, pre_material_topics, pre_lecture_methods, pre_lecture_kp, pre_questions, pre_question_kp_map, pre_skill_gaps source_lessons→pre_questions →(审核通过)→ knl_questions

5. 表结构字段（全集）

标记：PK=主键，FK=外键，UQ=唯一约束。仅列核心字段，JSONB 字段保留版本追溯。

usr_ 身份与学生
表核心字段说明
usr_users id(PK BIGSERIAL), openid(UQ VARCHAR64), nickname, parent_phone, created_at, updated_at 家长微信账号
usr_students id(PK BIGSERIAL), user_id(FK), name, grade(SMALLINT), avatar, is_default, onboarding_answers(JSONB), created_at, updated_at 学生档案；onboarding_answers 存初始问卷
knl_ 知识体系与题库
表核心字段说明
knl_systems id(PK), name(UQ), description, is_active 知识体系（如"小学数学竞赛体系"）
knl_modules id(PK), system_id(FK), name, description, exam_weight, olympiad_weight, sort_order 七大板块（固定7条）
knl_topics id(PK), module_id(FK), name, description, grade_start, grade_end, difficulty, sort_order 章节（如"第15讲 同余"）
knl_skills id(PK), topic_id(FK), name, grade_start, grade_end, difficulty, prerequisites(JSONB), error_types(JSONB) 知识点最小单元
knl_chapters id(PK SERIAL), code(UQ), name, subtitle, grade, sort_order, is_active, topic_id(FK→knl_topics) 执行态章节入口，兼容现有 API；逐步由 knl_topics 直接驱动
knl_questions id(PK BIGSERIAL), source_material, source_question_no, grade, difficulty, stem_latex, options(JSONB), answer_latex, solution_latex, trap_desc, error_hints, created_at 标准题库（无版权原文）；options 支持选择题
knl_question_skill_map PK(question_id FK, skill_id FK), is_primary, confidence 题目与知识点多对多
knl_question_solutions id(PK), question_id(FK), method_seq, title, summary, solution_latex, is_primary, created_at, UQ(question_id, method_seq) 一题多解
onb_ 评测 + 能力画像
表核心字段说明
onb_question_pools id(PK), grade, difficulty_band(high|mid|low), pool_name, version, is_active, created_at 评测题池（可运营维护）
onb_pool_question_map PK(pool_id FK, question_id FK), order_num 题池与题目映射
onb_ability_assessments id(PK), project_id(FK/UQ), intake_data(JSONB), ability_level, weak_points(JSONB), report_data(JSONB), calibrated(BOOL) 能力评估总档（一项目一份）
onb_assessment_sessions id(PK), assessment_id(FK), questions(JSONB), answers(JSONB), ai_grading(JSONB), completed_at 诊断会话（可多次）
onb_assessment_results id(PK), session_id(FK), question_id(FK), skill_id(FK), student_answer, correct_answer, is_correct, topic_score_contribution 逐题结果
onb_skill_mastery PK(student_id FK, skill_id FK), mastery_level, initial_score, target_score, current_score, updated_at 技能级掌握度（随作业结果持续回写）
onb_topic_scores PK(student_id FK, topic_id FK), score, trend, updated_at 章节雷达图得分
pla_ 项目与计划
表核心字段说明
pla_types id(PK), name, subject, grade_range, description, is_active 项目类型模板（如"华杯备赛"）
pla_learning_projects id(PK), student_id(FK), type_id(FK), target_date, goal_level, status, created_at 学生项目实例
pla_sprint_plans id(PK), project_id(FK/UQ), generated_at, confirmed_at, total_days, plan_data(JSONB), competition_name, goal_type, target_score, overall_level, daily_minutes 学习总计划（原 sprint_plans）
pla_milestones id(PK), sprint_plan_id(FK), seq, name, focus_topic_ids(JSONB), focus_skill_ids(JSONB), start_date, end_date, duration_days, score_before, score_target, score_after, status, completed_at, UQ(sprint_plan_id, seq) 里程碑节点
pla_milestone_retros id(PK), milestone_id(FK/UQ), checkin_days, task_completion_rate, score_before, score_after, q1_memorable_topic, q2_mastery_rating, q3_next_feeling, achievement_sent, created_at 里程碑复盘
pla_project_retros id(PK), project_id(FK/UQ), student_report(JSONB), platform_metrics(JSONB), generated_at 项目终局复盘
stu_ 学生执行与错题闭环
表核心字段说明
stu_training_plans id(PK), student_id(FK), sprint_plan_id(FK), milestone_id(FK), chapter_id(FK), plan_date, topic, key_points(JSONB), created_at, UQ(student_id, sprint_plan_id, plan_date) 日历计划（每日一行）
stu_training_plan_items id(PK), plan_id(FK), skill_id(FK), question_id(FK), item_type(new_practice|review), order_num, completed, completed_at 每日任务项（可关联具体题目）
stu_assignments id(PK), student_id(FK), chapter_id(FK), plan_date, image_url, image_url_thumb, status(assignment_status), total_count, correct_count, wrong_count, unknown_count, mood_text, created_at, updated_at 作业提交与批改状态
stu_problems id(PK), assignment_id(FK), seq, ocr_text, student_answer, correct_answer, result(problem_result), knowledge_point, trap_desc, solution_text, root_cause, review_status, review_stage, next_review_at, mastered_at, created_at 逐题判题结果（含 Ebbinghaus 复习）
stu_dialogues id(PK), problem_id(FK), role(ai|student), content, audio_url, created_at 题目级对话（旧表，向后兼容）
stu_ai_dialogues id(PK), student_id(FK), project_id(FK), context_type(VARCHAR32), context_id(BIGINT), role(ai|student), content, audio_url, created_at 统一 AI 对话（context_type：assessment_report / milestone_retro / problem_chat）
stu_review_sessions id(PK), assignment_id(FK/UQ), started_at, completed_at, summary_text, notified_parent, notified_at 作业复盘会话
stu_wrong_answer_skill_map PK(problem_id FK, skill_id FK), root_cause_type, diagnosed_at 错因归因映射
stu_daily_check_ins id(PK), student_id(FK), check_date(DATE), streak, created_at, UQ(student_id, check_date) 每日打卡与连续天数
pre_ 预处理库（独立部署，版权隔离）
表核心字段说明
pre_source_lessons id(PK), material_set, grade, level, semester, lesson_num, lesson_name, file_path, file_type, is_scanned, stage, created_at 原始教材索引（对应 31G 文件目录）
pre_material_topics id(PK), material_set, grade, level, topic_name, description, std_skill_ids(JSONB), mapped_at, created_at 材料自有主题（映射到 knl_skills）
pre_lecture_methods id(PK), lesson_id(FK), seq, title, summary, created_at, UQ(lesson_id, seq) 讲义"好方法"（解题策略）
pre_lecture_kp id(PK), lesson_id(FK), seq, title, content_latex, created_at, UQ(lesson_id, seq) 讲义知识点片段
pre_questions id(PK), lesson_id(FK), material_topic_id(FK), method_id(FK), question_type, grade, level, difficulty, stem_raw, answer_raw, solution_raw, stem_latex, answer_latex, solution_latex, options(JSONB), skill_ids_ai(JSONB), review_status(pending|approved|rejected), reviewer_note, reviewed_at, synced_to_std, synced_at, std_question_id, created_at 暂存题目（含完整审核与同步状态）
pre_question_kp_map PK(question_id FK, kp_id FK) 题目与讲义知识点多对多
pre_skill_gaps id(PK), material_set, description, question_count, suggested_skill, resolution, created_at 知识缺口候选（待补充到 knl_skills）

6. 统一约束建议

  • 域前缀强制隔离：跨域操作通过外键显式定义，不允许跨域直接 JOIN 无关表。
  • 多对多复合主键：knl_question_skill_map、stu_wrong_answer_skill_map、onb_pool_question_map、pre_question_kp_map 必须使用复合主键。
  • JSONB 快照字段：report_data、plan_data、student_report、onboarding_answers 保留完整版本，不做规范化分解。
  • chapters 兼容过渡：knl_chapters.topic_id → knl_topics.id；现有 API 继续用 chapters，逐步迁移到直接用 knl_topics。
  • 双轨评测题来源：默认按 grade + difficulty 规则筛题，关键项目绑定 onb_question_pools 固定题池。
  • 统一 AI 对话：stu_ai_dialogues.context_type 枚举：assessment_report / milestone_retro / problem_chat / plan_confirm。
  • 同步闸门严格：仅 pre_questions.review_status = 'approved' AND stem_latex IS NOT NULL 才可同步到 knl_questions。
  • 能力画像回写：onb_skill_mastery 和 onb_topic_scores 由作业批改后自动触发更新，不由前端写入。
